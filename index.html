Understanding TMJ

The temporomandibular joint acts as a sliding hinge connecting your jawbone to your skull. Symptoms include jaw pain, headaches, ear pain, and difficulty chewing. Early diagnosis leads to the best outcomes.

Common Symptoms

Jaw clicking · Facial pain · Headaches · Difficulty opening mouth · Ear ringing · Neck stiffness
